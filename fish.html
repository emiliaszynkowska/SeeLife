Angel Fish


Fun facts about angel fish!

    Angel Fish facts

  • Scientific Name : Pterophyllum

    		•

  • Class: Actinopterygii

    		•

  • Family: Cichlidae

  • Lifespan (in wild): 10 years

    		•

  • Weight: 56 ounces

  • Body size: up to 15cm

  • Colour: Orange, red, yellow, gold or silver

    		•

  • Diet: shrimp, white worms, bloodworms, meal worms, small insects and crustaceans

    		•

  • Habitat: shallow reefs in the tropical Atlantic, Indian, and mostly western Pacific Oceans
